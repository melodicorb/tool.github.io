Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug or issue with a tool?

You can report bugs or issues by using our contact form above or by sending an email directly to support@multitoolhub.com with details about the problem you're experiencing.

Are all the tools on MultiTool Hub free to use?

Yes, all the tools currently available on MultiTool Hub are completely free to use. We may introduce premium features in the future, but we'll always maintain a free tier.

How can I suggest a new tool for the platform?

We welcome tool suggestions! Please use our contact form or email us at info@multitoolhub.com with your idea, including what problem the tool would solve and any specific features you'd like to see.

What data does MultiTool Hub collect from users?

We collect minimal data necessary for the functioning of our tools. For specific details about our data collection practices, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
