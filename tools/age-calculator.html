How to Use the Age Calculator

1. Enter Your Date of Birth

Select your birth date using the date picker.

2. Choose Calculation Method

Select whether to calculate your age as of the current date and time or a custom date.

3. View Your Results

After clicking "Calculate Age", you'll see your age broken down into years, months, days, hours, and minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is my age calculated?

Our calculator uses precise date and time calculations to determine your exact age in years, months, days, hours, and minutes.

Why might my age calculation differ from other calculators?

Different calculators may use different methods for handling leap years, months with varying days, and time zones.

Is my birth date information stored?

Your calculation history is stored locally on your device only. No personal information is sent to our servers.

How accurate is the next birthday countdown?

The countdown is accurate to the day and accounts for leap years when calculating the days until your next birthday.
