About Audio Mixer

Our Audio Mixer is a powerful web-based tool that allows you to mix multiple audio tracks directly in your browser. Whether you're creating a podcast, mixing music tracks, or preparing audio for a video project, this tool provides essential mixing capabilities without the need for complex software.

Features

  • Mix multiple audio tracks simultaneously
  • Adjust volume and pan settings for each track
  • Visualize audio waveforms for precise editing
  • Solo and mute individual tracks
  • Apply basic effects like reverb and EQ
  • Export your final mix as an audio file
  • Works entirely in your browser - no software installation required

How to Use

  1. Click "Add Track" to upload audio files from your device
  2. Adjust volume and pan settings for each track using the sliders
  3. Use the solo and mute buttons to isolate or silence specific tracks
  4. Apply effects as needed to enhance your mix
  5. Use the master volume control to adjust the overall output level
  6. Click "Play All" to preview your mix
  7. When satisfied, click "Export Mix" to download the final audio file

Frequently Asked Questions

What audio file formats are supported?

The Audio Mixer supports common audio formats including MP3, WAV, OGG, and AAC files.

Is there a limit to how many tracks I can mix?

For optimal performance, we recommend mixing up to 8 tracks simultaneously. Adding more tracks may affect performance depending on your device.

Can I save my project and continue later?

Currently, the Audio Mixer doesn't have a project save feature. We recommend completing your mix in one session and exporting the result.

What format is the exported mix?

Your final mix will be exported as a high-quality WAV file, which you can later convert to other formats if needed.

Does this tool work on mobile devices?

Yes, the Audio Mixer is designed to work on both desktop and mobile devices, though a larger screen provides a better mixing experience.
