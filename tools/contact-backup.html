How to Use the Contact Backup Tool

Importing Contacts

Select your import format (CSV or vCard), then upload your file and click "Import Contacts". The tool will parse your file and display your contacts in the table.

Managing Contacts

You can add new contacts using the "Add New Contact" button. Edit existing contacts by clicking the edit icon, or delete contacts with the delete icon.

Exporting Contacts

Choose your desired export format (CSV, vCard, or JSON) and click "Export Contacts" to download your contact list in that format.

Frequently Asked Questions

What contact formats are supported?

This tool supports importing contacts from CSV and vCard (.vcf) files. You can export to CSV, vCard, or JSON formats.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your contact data is never sent to any server or stored online.

Can I edit contacts after importing?

Absolutely. You can edit any contact by clicking the edit icon, or add new contacts using the "Add New Contact" button.

What CSV format should I use?

Your CSV should have columns for name, phone, and email. The tool will attempt to detect these columns automatically.
