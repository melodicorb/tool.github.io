How to Use the Grammar Corrector

  1. Type or paste your text in the input area above.
  2. Click the "Check Grammar" button to analyze your text.
  3. Review the highlighted errors and suggested corrections.
  4. Click on a suggestion to apply it to your text.
  5. Use the "Copy Corrected Text" button to copy the improved version.

Features

  • Spelling error detection and correction
  • Grammar mistake identification
  • Punctuation error correction
  • Style improvement suggestions
  • Detailed explanations for each correction

FAQ

Is my text stored or shared?

No, your text is processed locally in your browser and is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

What languages are supported?

Currently, our grammar checker supports English text only. We plan to add support for more languages in the future.

Are there any text length limitations?

The tool works best with texts up to 5,000 characters. Longer texts may be processed in chunks for optimal performance.
