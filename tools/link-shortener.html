About Link Shortener

Our Link Shortener tool allows you to transform long, unwieldy URLs into short, easy-to-share links. Whether you're sharing links on social media, in emails, or in print materials, our tool makes your links more manageable and professional.

Features

  • Create short, memorable links from long URLs
  • Customize your link with a custom alias
  • Set expiration dates for temporary links
  • Track the number of clicks on your shortened links
  • Generate QR codes for your shortened links
  • View and manage all your shortened links in one place
  • Copy links to clipboard with one click

How to Use

  1. Enter the long URL you want to shorten in the input field
  2. Optionally, customize your link with a custom alias
  3. Select an expiration time for your link (or choose "Never")
  4. Click the "Shorten" button to generate your short link
  5. Copy the shortened link to share it, or generate a QR code
  6. Track clicks and manage your links in the history section

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the shortened links permanent?

By default, links don't expire unless you set an expiration date. You can choose to have links expire after 1 day, 7 days, 30 days, or 90 days.

Can I customize my shortened links?

Yes, you can create custom aliases for your links to make them more memorable or branded.

Is there a limit to how many links I can create?

There is no limit to the number of links you can create with our free tool.

Can I track how many people click my links?

Yes, our tool automatically tracks the number of clicks on each of your shortened links.

Are the shortened links secure?

Yes, our link shortener maintains the security of the original link. If your original URL uses HTTPS, the shortened link will also direct to the secure site.
