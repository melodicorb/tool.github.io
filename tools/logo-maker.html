About Logo Maker

Our Logo Maker is a simple yet powerful tool that allows you to create professional-looking logos for your brand, business, or personal project. With an intuitive interface, you can design custom logos by combining shapes, text, and colors - all directly in your browser, with no design experience required.

Features

  • Multiple shape options (circle, square, triangle, star)
  • Customizable text with various font options
  • Full color control for shapes, text, and background
  • Real-time preview of your logo design
  • Download your finished logo as a PNG image
  • Simple, intuitive interface for beginners
  • No account or sign-up required

How to Use

  1. Select a shape for your logo from the shapes panel
  2. Enter your brand or company name in the text field
  3. Choose a font style and adjust the text size
  4. Select colors for your shape, text, and background
  5. Preview your logo in real-time as you make changes
  6. When satisfied with your design, click the Download button to save your logo

Frequently Asked Questions

What file format will my logo be downloaded as?

Your logo will be downloaded as a PNG (Portable Network Graphics) file, which maintains good quality and supports transparency.

Can I use my logo for commercial purposes?

Yes! Logos created with our tool are free for both personal and commercial use. You own full rights to your created designs.

Can I upload my own images or icons?

The current version doesn't support custom image uploads, but we're considering adding this feature in future updates.

What resolution will my logo be?

Logos are generated at 1000x1000 pixels, which is suitable for most digital uses including websites, social media, and email signatures.

Can I save my logo design and edit it later?

Currently, the logo maker doesn't have a save feature. We recommend downloading your logo if you need to take a break and want to save your progress.
