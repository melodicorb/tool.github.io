About Notepad

Our online Notepad is a simple yet powerful tool designed to help you quickly jot down notes, ideas, or any text content. With features like auto-save, formatting options, and the ability to download your notes, it's perfect for everyday use.

Features

  • Clean, distraction-free writing environment
  • Auto-save functionality to prevent data loss
  • Basic text formatting options (bold, italic, underline)
  • Ability to download your notes as a text file
  • Copy to clipboard with one click
  • Character and word count
  • Mobile-friendly interface for note-taking on the go

How to Use

  1. Simply start typing in the notepad area
  2. Use the toolbar buttons to format your text
  3. Your notes are automatically saved as you type
  4. Click the Download button to save your notes as a text file
  5. Use the Copy button to copy all text to your clipboard
  6. Click New to start a fresh note (your previous note will be saved)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Your notes are stored locally in your browser's storage and are not sent to any server. They remain private on your device.

Will I lose my notes if I close the browser?

No, your notes are automatically saved to your browser's local storage. They will be available when you return to the notepad, even after closing your browser.

Is there a character limit?

The notepad can handle thousands of characters, but for very large documents, we recommend downloading your text periodically and using a dedicated word processor.

Can I format my text?

Yes, you can use the formatting buttons in the toolbar to make text bold, italic, or underlined. You can also change the font size.

How do I save my notes?

Your notes are automatically saved as you type. You can also download them as a text file by clicking the Download button.
