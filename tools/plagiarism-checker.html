How to Use the Plagiarism Checker

  1. Type or paste your text in the input area above.
  2. Select the type of check you want to perform.
  3. Optionally, specify sources to exclude from the check.
  4. Click the "Check Plagiarism" button to analyze your text.
  5. Review the plagiarism score and matched sources.
  6. Download a detailed report for your records.

FAQ

Is my text stored or shared?

No, your text is processed securely and is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties after the check is complete.

How accurate is the plagiarism detection?

Our tool compares your text against billions of web pages, academic papers, and publications for high accuracy. However, no plagiarism checker is 100% perfect, so we recommend using it as a guide.

Are there any text length limitations?

The standard check supports texts up to 3,000 words. For longer documents, you may need to check sections separately or use the deep analysis option.
